The purpose is to verify correctness of the probabilistic reasoning and identify any potential failures in the implementation.

## Root Cause Analysis
The probabilistic reasoning is correctly implemented:
1. Actuator selection follows the 50/50 distribution (lines 5-6)
2. Wind effects properly condition probabilities on wind strength (lines 8-10)
3. Position calculations correctly combine deterministic and probabilistic components (lines 24-34)
4. Probability rules are correctly applied (P(¬A)=1−P(A) for legal position checks)

Key correct behaviors:
```prolog

% Correct conditional probability for wind effects

```
